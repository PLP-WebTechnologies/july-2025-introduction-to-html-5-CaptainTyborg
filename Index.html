About Us

We are a passionate group of students and professionals working to protect the Earth through sustainable projects and community action.

  • Vision: A green, sustainable Earth for all.
  • Values: Sustainability, Community, Innovation, Responsibility

Our Projects

🌳 GreenRoots

We plant trees in schools and communities every month to fight deforestation and beautify neighborhoods.

🧼 Clean Rivers Campaign

Volunteers gather monthly to remove waste from riverbanks and protect aquatic ecosystems.

📚 EcoEd

We deliver workshops and interactive sessions in schools to teach kids about recycling, climate change, and nature.
